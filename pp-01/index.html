Typefaces:

  • Raleway
  • Playfair Display

Playfair Display is inspired by early 18th century European early technology, including printers. This typeface is an excellent attention grabber as a title, so it works great with Raleway. The counterpart, Raleway, comes from sans-serif's family with a modern vibe. It is said it was designed to be more geometric, which matches well with the high-contrast, fluid lines of Playfair.
